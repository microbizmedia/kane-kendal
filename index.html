Lower Back Pain Holding You Back? Let’s Fix It.

Lower back pain is one of the most common issues we see—and it can affect everything from work to sleep. It often starts from muscle strain, disc problems, or irritation in the joints and nerves of your spine. At Kane & Kendall Spine Management, we focus on finding the exact cause of your pain so we can treat it the right way—not just temporarily relieve it.

  • Treatment & Prevention

If your pain isn’t improving, we use targeted treatments to reduce inflammation and relieve nerve pressure. Your care plan may include injections, therapy, and chiropractic care to restore proper movement. We also help prevent future pain by improving posture, strengthening your body, and guiding you on everyday habits like lifting, sleeping, and staying active.

Leg Pain? The Problem Might Not Be Your Leg.

Leg pain can come from many different sources—including your lower back. Nerve irritation, muscle strain, or joint issues can all cause pain that travels down the leg. That’s why treating the symptom alone doesn’t work. We focus on identifying where the pain is really coming from so we can fix it at the source.

  • Treatment & Prevention

We start with a full evaluation and, if needed, diagnostic testing. Treatment may include spinal adjustments, therapy, and soft tissue work to relieve tension and improve movement. We also help you prevent future issues by improving flexibility, strength, and daily movement habits.

Sharp Pain Down Your Leg?
It Could Be Sciatica.

Sciatica happens when the sciatic nerve is irritated or compressed, causing pain that travels from your lower back down into your leg. It may feel sharp, burning, or like tingling or numbness—usually on one side. Without proper treatment, it can become a long-term problem.

  • Treatment & Prevention

We focus on relieving pressure on the nerve and restoring proper alignment. Your treatment may include chiropractic care, physical therapy, and targeted pain relief options. We also guide you on posture, movement, and lifestyle changes to help prevent flare-ups and keep you pain-free.

Spinal Curvature? Early Care Makes a Difference.

Scoliosis is a condition where the spine curves or twists abnormally. It often develops at a young age, but it can continue affecting posture, comfort, and overall health over time. Early detection is key to preventing the condition from getting worse.

  • Treatment & Prevention

We monitor spinal alignment and recommend the right level of care based on the severity. Treatment may include posture-focused therapy, exercises, and supportive care to improve strength and stability. Our goal is to help maintain proper alignment and prevent long-term complications.

Shoulder Pain Making Everyday Tasks Hard?

Shoulder pain can make simple movements like lifting, reaching, or even sleeping uncomfortable. It’s often caused by strain, overuse, or joint and muscle issues that worsen over time. The key to recovery is understanding what’s causing it.

  • Treatment & Prevention

We create a personalized plan that may include chiropractic adjustments, therapy, and soft tissue treatments to relieve tension and restore movement. With early treatment, most patients recover faster and avoid long-term problems. We also guide you on how to protect your shoulder during daily activities.
